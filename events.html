    Reunion Luncheon - Classes of 1965, 1960, 1955, 1950, and 1945
    12:00 p.m.
    Borghese Room
    Hilton Philadelphia City Avenue
    4200 City Avenue
    Philadelphia, PA
    Join your classmates for a special luncheon for our honored reunion guests- the 75th, 70th, 65th, 60th, 55th, and 50-year reunion celebrations. Don’t miss this exciting occasion to acknowledge the “pioneers” of our osteopathic profession. Each year PCOM honors the 50th anniversary class - the Class of 1965.
